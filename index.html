About Me

I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Vision Lab (CVLab), École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL). Before that, I received bachelor's degree and master's degree in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, with focus on multi-view geometry and point cloud analysis.
