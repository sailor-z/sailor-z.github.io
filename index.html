About Me

I am a fifth-year Ph.D student at Computer Vision Lab (CVLab), École polytechnique fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), supervised by Prof. Pascal Fua and Dr. Mathieu Salzmann. Before that, I completed my bachelor's and master's degrees at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). My research is centered around enhancing robustness and generalization in 3D computer vision, with a specific focus on 3D interaction, 3D understanding, and 3D reconstruction.
I am actively seeking opportunities for a postdoctoral position starting in 2025.
